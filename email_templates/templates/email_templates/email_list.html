Email templates

Steps for importing to SendGrid:

  1. Create an email template in the Django admin.
  2. Come to this page and click on the template you created.
  3. Right-click the page and click "view source".
  4. Copy the entire code into the CSS inline tool and run the tool.
  5. Copy the output and paste into the SendGrid template.
