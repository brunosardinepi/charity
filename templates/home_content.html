Our mission

PageFund provides an online platform for nonprofits, schools, organizations, companies, and individuals to quickly and easily raise extra funds by allowing anyone to campaign on their behalf.

Pages

Pages are fixed spaces for anyone from nonprofits to individuals that are looking to raise extra funds. Use a Page to tell a story to the world, solicit direct donations, and post updates to Subscribers, all in one place.

Campaigns

Campaigns provide a goal-based means for anyone to fundraise for any Page. Campaigns are simple to setup, provide multiple options, and all donations are passed directly to the Page they are benefiting.
