How it works

Pages are perpetual locations for anyone that is looking to raise extra funds for their own organization or personal cause. A Page can be used to tell a story to the world, solicit direct donations, and post pictures, all in one place!

Campaigns are goal-based fundraisers, with a beginning and an end, that are designed for anyone to raise funds on behalf of any organization, individual, or cause that they are passionate about. Campaigns don't require any affiliation with the organization or cause and will funnel donations directly to the Page they are benefiting, without having to manually exchange funds!

To donate to a Page or a Campaign, search for it on PageFund and click "Donate". Select the amount you wish to donate and enter your payment details. Sign up for a free account on PageFund to save your payment details for future use, or to set up a monthly recurring donation. Your donation will then be sent securely and directly to the Page!
