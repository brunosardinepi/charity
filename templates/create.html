How it works

Pages are fixed locations for anyone that is looking to raise extra funds for their cause. Use a Page to tell a story to the world, solicit direct donations, and post pictures, all in one place.

Campaigns are goal-based fundraisers designed for anyone to raise funds on behalf of any Page. Campaigns donâ€™t require any affiliation, are easy to setup, and funnel donations directly to the Page they are benefiting without having to exchange funds.

To donate to a Page or a Campaign, search for it on PageFund and click "Donate" when on the Page or Campaign. Select the amount you want to donate and enter your payment details. Sign up for a free account on PageFund to save your payment details for future use or to set up a monthly recurring donation. Your donation will then be securely sent directly to the Page!
